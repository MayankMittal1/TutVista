Hola students and parents!

TutVista is a free online problem-solving and doubt-clearing platform created in 2021 by Hypertext Assassins, a development and coding team from IIT Roorkee comprising of Mayank Mittal, Abhinav Agarwal, Divyansh Bhadauria and Srinjoy Ghosh.

TutVista has a team of competent teachers in the fields of Physics, Chemistry and Mathematics who excel in offering the best educational guidance and are ready to devote their time to solve the doubts of thousands of students throughout the country. We at TutVista aim to provide a free platform to students of classes 9-12 to ask their doubts, both of school-level and of competitive exams like JEE and NEET, in the Covid-Era, where the students are facing various difficulties in online education and are not being able to have a complete and confident grasp on their subjects due to limitations of online tutoring during the ongoing pandemic.

As the founders themselves were students preparing for JEE during the pandemic-induced crisis, they had first hand experiences of the tension and anxiety that unsolved doubts causes in a student’s mind. Hence, TutVista was an initiative by them to give the help, that they couldn’t receive, to the current crop of JEE and NEET aspirants.

Well good luck for your studies and keep asking questions! For more queries you can contact us at tutvista@gmail.com
